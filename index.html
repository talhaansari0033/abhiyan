About Clean India

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is a nation-wide campaign in India for the period 2014 to 2019 that aimed to clean the streets, roads and infrastructure of the country's cities, towns, and rural areas.

Our Mission

The mission is to promote cleanliness, hygiene and eliminate open defecation across India through community engagement and infrastructure improvements.

How You Can Help

  • Don't litter. Use dustbins.
  • Participate in community cleaning drives.
  • Raise awareness about cleanliness in your locality.
  • Volunteer with NGOs promoting hygiene and sanitation.
